The CS2 Austin Major!

Illinois Tech Esports Boardmember

Running a student organization for esports funded by the school is both an exciting leadership opportunity and a serious responsibility. With financial backing, there's the ability to provide members with quality equipment, tournament entry fees, and promotional materials, creating a professional and inclusive environment. Managing the organization involves coordinating competitive teams, scheduling practices, organizing events, and promoting campus engagement. It also means balancing competitive goals with academics, ensuring students grow both in-game and in leadership. Working closely with school administration teaches communication, budgeting, and accountability. Building a supportive community around shared gaming interests creates lasting friendships and fosters collaboration. It’s incredibly rewarding to watch players improve, represent the school in tournaments, and contribute to a growing and respected culture of esports. More than just gaming, it becomes a platform for leadership, inclusion, and innovation—giving students the chance to be part of something impactful and forward-thinking within the campus community.

Oath Esports Youth Valorant Coach

Youth coaching is about more than teaching skills—it’s about mentoring, building confidence, and creating a positive foundation for growth. At Oath Esports, youth coaching plays a vital role in shaping young gamers into well-rounded individuals. Through structured training, teamwork, and a supportive environment, players not only improve their in-game performance but also learn discipline, communication, and resilience. Coaches at Oath focus on developing character as much as mechanics, emphasizing sportsmanship and healthy competition. By blending fun with guidance, youth coaching at Oath Esports empowers the next generation to thrive both in and out of the game.

Alleyrats CS2 Club

The AlleyRat CS2 Club was founded to foster organized, competitive Counter-Strike 2 play in a welcoming and structured environment. From its inception, the club prioritized inclusive team-building, clear rules, and regular practice schedules to help players grow their skills and enjoy the game. By organizing weekly scrimmages, hosting mini-tournaments, and offering coaching support, AlleyRat provides a platform for both casual and serious gamers to connect and improve. The emphasis on fair play, communication, and strategy creates a healthy competitive atmosphere. Since launching, the club has attracted enthusiasts across skill levels, uniting them through shared passion and a commitment to elevating their game.

E4E Instructor

Teaching an entrepreneurship and esports mentorship program for high school seniors is a unique opportunity to blend innovation with passion. This program empowers students to explore the business side of esports—developing skills in branding, content creation, marketing, and team management. Through mentorship, students gain real-world insights from industry professionals, while also learning how to turn their gaming interests into potential careers or startups. It encourages leadership, creativity, and critical thinking, all within a space that excites them. By the end, seniors walk away with not only practical experience but also the confidence to pursue future opportunities in esports and beyond.
